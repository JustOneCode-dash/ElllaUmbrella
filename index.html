General Information

Magubahay is a vibrant barangay situated in the municipality of Batan, Aklan. As of the 2020 Census, it is home to 501 residents.

Community Profile

The community is characterized by its lush landscapes and agricultural heritage. With a median age of 24, Magubahay possesses a dynamic youth sector.
